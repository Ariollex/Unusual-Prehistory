A large lawless fish from the Devonian, these fish act similarly to stingrays. Instead of a stinger however they rely on a their tough armored
bodies to protect against would be predators. A unique and unexpected aspect of this animal is the saliva inside of its mouth. This saliva
has fairly active bioelectrical aspects to them. The use for this is uncertain, possibly a defense tactic or some sort of skimmming method for food.
However, if one uses a flask on their mouth, they can get a bottle of the stuff.
