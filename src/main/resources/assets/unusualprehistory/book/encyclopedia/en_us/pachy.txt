These small dinosaurs from the Cretaceous have quite the attitude despite their relatively small size. While they will
tolerate nearby people, after some time they will begin to attack. They unleash a flurry of attacks ranging from headbutts,
head slams, to even kicking.
<NEWLINE>
However, if you give a pachy some raw ginkgo seeds, they will become passive for much longer time along
with granting you with Pachys Might. This effect increases attack knockback by a wide margin.
