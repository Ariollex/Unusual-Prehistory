Disgusting abominations from the mezosoic era, these beasts are coated in a sticky sap-like substance. Unlike most other
revived animals, these creatures seem to have been shaped by their preservation, with the sap seemingly changing the genetic
makeup. These vile creatures attack through both stabbing with their proboscis and launching a sappy substance at high speeds.
<NEWLINE>
Despite their corrupted nature, their abdomen acts as a good way to store blood. This can be refitted and crafted into a
DNA Fridge, allowing for mass storage of DNA in a compressed container.
