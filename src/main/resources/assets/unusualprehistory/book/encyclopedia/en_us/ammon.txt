A rather broad group of cephalopods that lived all throughout time, only going extinct during the end of the cretaceous
period. Like many smaller aquatic animals, they can be bucketed.
<NEWLINE>
<NEWLINE>
<NEWLINE>
On death these animals drop shell shards, which can be used to recreate
an ammonite shell or they can be made into a war pick. War picks act as both a way to mine and engage in combat.
