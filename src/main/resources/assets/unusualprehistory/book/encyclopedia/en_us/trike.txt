Hailing from the Cretaceous, these large herbivores dominated their niche during their time. These large herbivores reined supreme as they could
hold their own against most large predators, including the formidable Tyrannosaurus Rex. These dinosaurs are docile unless provoked. When aggressive they will
steamroll any would be attackers.

They can be fed Ginkgo fruits to tame them. When ridden these massive creatures are fairly fast, allowing them to plow down
trees with ease. This makes them ample wood harvesters.
