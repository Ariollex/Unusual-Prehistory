A small lungfish from the Devonian period, these small fish don't offer much outside of meat and decoration.
Like many smaller aquatic animals, they can be bucketed. While not ver useful these fish are favored by dunkleosteus,
being able to be to passify the monsterous beasts. One must use a golden scaumenacia to passify them.
