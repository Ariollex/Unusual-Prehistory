A small lungfish from the Devonian period, these small fish don't offer much outside of meat and decoration.
Like many smaller aquatic animals, they can be bucketed. While not very useful these fish are favored by dunkleosteus,
being able to be to pacify the monstrous beasts. One must use a golden scaumenacia to passive them.
